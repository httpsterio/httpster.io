Considering how big of an impact dancing games have had on my life, I'm actually surprised it's taken me this long to write anything about them.

Getting in to the game, the scene and meeting the people of the community is one of the most formative aspects in my life and has shaped the direction my life has taken dramatically.

So, I thought I'd open up a bit about my journey!

## What are dancing games?

I realize that _"dancing games"_ doesn't really mean a ton to most people and other people might think of games like Just Dance or Dance Rush with the shuffling. When I talk about dancing games, I mean specifically _"arrow"_ games played with a four or five panel dance pad. Games like Dance Dance Revolution or in my case, In The Groove and Stepmania.
